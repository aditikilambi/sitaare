about

UCSD'S PREMIER SOUTH ASIAN A CAPPELLA TEAM

Sitaare is UCSD’s premier award-winning South Asian A Cappella team. Since its inception in 2006, Sitaare has taken pride in its diverse vocal talent, continuing to explore ways to showcase variety and creativity in their repertoire. Sitaare enjoys performing different genres of both Indian and Western music, from Bollywood and Indian classical, to R&B and pop.

In addition to UCSD’s cultural events, Sitaare has had the opportunity to perform at community events, weddings, and corporate events in San Diego, the Bay Area, and beyond.

Sitaare always looks to push the boundaries of desi a cappella and reach for the stars.

auditions 2019

sing. perform. travel. compete. win.

Join our family

Do you love music? If so you're in the right place. Sitaare is our home away from home and we're super excited to hold our 2019 auditions.

We're looking for talented singers and beatboxers. No musical experience is necessary. Our team members come from all musical backgrounds, from high school choir to Carnatic classical to shower singing. There is no doubt that we can make a place for you here.

How to audition:

  1. Prepare a 30 second to 1 minute long solo in whatever style you are most comfortable (western, hindi, classical, whatever!). If you are interested in beatboxing, prepare a 1 minute sample of your beats.
  2. Sign up for an audition here or just walk in!
  3. Come meet us at your audition time. We'll ask for your solo and do a couple pitch exercises.
  4. If we are impressed with your audition, we will personally invite you to callbacks, where we will teach you a piece from our 2019 competition set!

Best of luck and we can't wait to meet you!
