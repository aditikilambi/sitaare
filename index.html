about

UCSD'S PREMIER SOUTH ASIAN A CAPPELLA TEAM

Sitaare is UCSD’s premier award-winning South Asian A Cappella team. Since its inception in 2006, Sitaare has taken pride in its diverse vocal talent, continuing to explore ways to showcase variety and creativity in their repertoire. Sitaare enjoys performing different genres of both Indian and Western music, from Bollywood and Indian classical, to R&B and pop.

In addition to UCSD’s cultural events, Sitaare has had the opportunity to perform at community events, weddings, and corporate events in San Diego, the Bay Area, and beyond.

Sitaare always looks to push the boundaries of desi a cappella and reach for the stars.

Join our family

Welcome to Sitaare Virtual Auditions! We're very excited to see your lovely faces and hear everyone's lovely voices! No experience is needed to audition! We're looking for people in all voice parts and beatboxing. If you are unable to sign up currently, walk in auditions are welcome as well!

How to Audition

We will be holding our 2021 auditions on Saturday 1/9, Monday 1/11, Tuesday 1/12, and Wednesday 1/13. Please consult the flyer that is on the left side of this page for the audition times.

If you would like to audition for Sitaare this year please fill out this form to sign up!

Audition Format

The virtual audition take place over zoom and will be around 15 minutes. It will consist of:

  • Warm-ups/vocal test
  • Pitch matching
  • Rhythm Tests
  • 1 min solo song (English or South Asian) and/or beatbox solo

Best of luck and we can't wait to meet you!
